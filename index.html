Roadmap

Phase 0 (Week 0?1): Immediate Cash, Proof of Action Goal: First $500
  1. Flip free ? cash: list 10 free items/day (local classifieds), relist with better photos, deliver. Target: 3 flips/week at $50?$150 margin.
  2. Service arbitrage: offer simple gigs (logo cleanups, profile banners, podcast shownotes) using free tools; subcontract for delivery.
  3. Street-level lead gen: walk-in pitches to 20 local businesses/day (window signage, menu redesign, QR ordering setup). Offer ?pay on delivery?.
  4. Public log: daily thread documenting actions and outcomes to attract inbound.
Phase 1 (Week 2?4): Productized Service Goal: $3,000/mo run-rate
  1. Pick one pain: appointment-setting for solo pros, local SEO quick wins, AI summaries for YouTubers, Shopify speed tune-ups.
  2. Define offer: fixed scope, fast delivery, money-back guarantee, weekly reporting.
  3. Acquire with volume: 100 targeted cold emails/day using free enrichment, 20 DMs/day, 10 Loom audits/week.
  4. Delivery SOP: build templatized workflows; outsource repeatables to vetted freelancers; keep margin > 60%.
Phase 2 (Month 2?4): Scale Systems Goal: $10k?$30k/mo
  1. Channel focus: keep 1?2 channels that work; discard the rest ruthlessly.
  2. Hire bench: 3?5 freelancers with clear SLAs; QA checklist; async-first.
  3. Raise price: add premium tier with guaranteed outcomes or performance components.
  4. Systemize: weekly operations review; scorecards; simple CRM; cash buffer target of 2 months expenses.
Phase 3 (Month 4?9): Build Assets Goal: $50k?$80k/mo
  1. Productize knowledge: mini-course or playbook built from your SOPs; bundle with templates.
  2. Micro-SaaS or automation: narrowly scoped tool that your clients already pay for manually.
  3. Distribution loops: affiliates, referral incentives, co-promotions, content repurposing flywheel.
  4. Improve unit economics: churn reduction, expansion revenue, upsells.
Phase 4 (Month 9?18): Compounding to $1M Goal: $1M cumulative revenue
  1. Concentrate winners: double-down on top 20% offers/channels producing 80% of revenue.
  2. Operational excellence: simple dashboard, cashflow discipline, monthly experiments cadence.
  3. Optional scale: outbound team, paid acquisition (reinvest profits), partnerships.
  4. Resilience: diversify 2+ revenue streams, maintain emergency buffer, keep fixed costs lean.
